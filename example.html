Pro Tips from This Example

  • 1. The header uses a gradient background to make it more interesting and eye-catching
  • 2. Each card has a colored left border to add visual interest and divide the sections
  • 3. Buttons have hover effects so users know they can click on them
  • 4. The max-w-6xl keeps the content from being too wide on big screens
  • 5. Shadow effects (shadow-lg, shadow-2xl) make elements look like they are floating
  • 6. Proper spacing with p- and m- classes makes the design look organized and clean
